Spread Education, Spread Love

Spread Education, Spread Love is a limited collection launched by estuDAR Youth Association. Created to promote a wave of awareness by reinforcing the importance of education and love, and the powerful impact that arises when these two elements converge. By collecting school supplies, we aim to promote the education and academic success of the young people we support, ensuring they have the tools needed for a prosperous future and seamless integration into professional life.

The design embraces a clean and minimalist style, highlighting the power of visual storytelling to emphasize the connection between education and love. Each visual element is thoughtfully designed to align with the campaign's mission, celebrating the cultural heritage of Portuguese-speaking countries while drawing attention to the importance of educational support for the youth.
